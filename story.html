A little bit of my story.

What if technology didn’t scare people, what if it helped people?

The world would be a better place.

My First Business

As I grew up, I noticed a theme. People were scared of the very tools that empowered them. We rarely love what we don’t understand. In middle school, I made a business out of training people how to use a computer and building websites and networks to help them succeed in their small businesses and personal work.

High School

In high school, I began to write code and hone my design skills I always took design very seriously because I knew no matter how sophisticated the tool, if it was unusable, it was worthless.

When the iPhone was announced in 2007, I was inspired to design and prototype my own portable device, which designed UI and a system architecture for.

College and Beyond

I chose to pursue a degree in Computer Science because I believe in the power of software to improve the lives of people around the planet. I believe that computing should “just work” and that technology should integrate seamlessly with ones daily life. While working in IT in college, I observed first hand the challenges people have with computer and I am inspired to be apart of building elegant, user friendly solutions.

We can’t settle with the way things are. To push humanity forward we must build better tools that delight and inspire. Things can be better, and I can build them.
